Robert R. Johnson Branding

Robert R. Johnson, Inc. are buyers and sellers of U.S. and world coins, stamps, autographs, banknotes, and other historical documents. They offer full appraisal services, conduct public and online auctions and handle private treaty sales. The goal was to refresh the Robert R. Johnson brand with a new logo and website.
