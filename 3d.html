My first Blender render. Blender is a free 3D modeling tool that can be used to create and animate models for AR and VR. About

After 10 years of working in print design, web design, and interface design, I decided to explore design outside the two dimensional surface. This is my collection of exercises and experiments with new three-dimensional design tools and resources. I believe the future of design is beyond the screen and in our environment, and I am here to explore how to bridge this gap.
