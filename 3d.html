A-Frame World

My first virtual world created with A-Frame WebVR library. This was an exercise to play around with A-frame primitives and experiment with the environment and animation components.

Blender Tutorial

My first Blender render. Blender is a free 3D modeling tool that can be used to create and animate models for AR and VR. It was my first time working with 3D modeling, and it was an interesting (and challenging) exercise. Thank you to Andrew Price and his Blender Beginner Youtube tutorials for a great overview of the software.

About

After 10 years of working in print design, web design, and interface design, I decided to explore design outside the two dimensional surface. This is my collection of exercises and experiments with new 3D design tools and resources. I believe the future of design is beyond the screen and in our environment, and I am here to explore how to bridge this gap.

Follow @kreatyvdesign for updates.
